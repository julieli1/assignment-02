Lisa Anthony

I work on understanding, designing and developing natural user interactions for children, especially pen, touch, and gesture interaction, for applications in education, fitness, and serious games.

Contributions

Lisa Anthony is the director of the Intelligent Natural Interaction Technology Laboratory (INIT Lab). The INIT Lab. Her research focuses on natural interactions for children, ranging from touchscreen interaction, whole-body interaction, and multimodal interaction


History

Lisa Anthony has a Bachelor of Science and a Master of Science in computer science with official concentrations in artificial intelligence, human-computer Interaction, and software engineering at Drexel University. She earned her Ph.D. from the Human Computer Interaction Institute in the School of Computer Science at Carnegie Mellon University in 2008.
